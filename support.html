Shipping and Delivery Policy (for Digital Services)

As Freelogic.in is a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) product, "Shipping and Delivery" refers to the provisioning and access to our digital services.

  • Service Access: Upon successful subscription and payment confirmation (post-launch), your Freelogic.in account will be activated, and you will gain access to the features corresponding to your chosen plan. Access is typically granted immediately or within a few minutes.
  • Account Credentials: You will receive your login credentials (or instructions to set them up) via the email address provided during signup. It is your responsibility to keep these credentials secure.
  • No Physical Shipping: There are no physical goods to be shipped. All services are delivered digitally through our web platform.
  • Service Availability: We strive to ensure high availability of our services, subject to planned maintenance and unforeseen technical issues, as outlined in our Terms & Conditions.

Cancellation and Refund Policy

This policy outlines how cancellations and refunds are handled for Freelogic.in subscriptions (applicable post-launch).

Please read this section carefully. Our full Cancellation and Refund terms will also be detailed in our Terms & Conditions upon launch.

Subscription Cancellation:

  • User-Initiated Cancellation: You can cancel your Freelogic.in subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting our support team.
  • Effect of Cancellation:
    • If you cancel a monthly subscription, your access to the paid features will continue until the end of your current paid billing cycle. You will not be charged for subsequent months.
    • If you cancel an annual subscription, your access to paid features will continue until the end of your current paid annual term.
  • No Pro-Rata Refunds for Mid-Cycle Cancellations: Generally, we do not offer pro-rata refunds for subscriptions cancelled mid-billing cycle (monthly or annual).

Refunds:

  • General Policy: Except when required by law, paid subscription fees for Freelogic.in are non-refundable. We encourage users to utilize any free trial period (if offered at launch) to evaluate the service before committing to a paid subscription.
  • Exceptional Circumstances: Refund requests due to exceptional circumstances (e.g., significant service unavailability not rectified in a timely manner, billing errors on our part) will be considered on a case-by-case basis. To request a refund under such circumstances, please contact our support team at srikar.rao@freelogic.in with detailed information.
  • Timeframe for Refund Requests: Any requests for refunds under exceptional circumstances must typically be made within a specific period (e.g., 7-14 days) of the transaction or issue occurring. This will be specified in the full Terms & Conditions.
  • Processing: Approved refunds will be processed back to the original method of payment within a certain number of business days (e.g., 5-10 business days), subject to payment processor timelines.

We reserve the right to modify this Cancellation and Refund Policy. Any changes will be posted on this page and, where appropriate, notified to you by email. Continued use of the service after such changes will constitute your consent to the revised policy.

For full details, please refer to our Terms & Conditions.
