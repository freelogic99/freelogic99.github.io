Support Information

How We Can Help

We are committed to providing helpful support for Freelogic.in users. This page outlines how you can get assistance and what our support covers. As Freelogic.in is currently under development, support options will expand upon official launch.

Contact Methods

For now, and for general inquiries about the upcoming Freelogic.in CRM:

  • Email: srikarayilneni@gmail.com (Preferred for detailed inquiries)
  • Contact Page: You can also reach out via our Contact Page.

Upon launch, we plan to offer dedicated support channels for subscribed users, which may include in-app support or a help desk system.

Response Times (Post-Launch Expectation)

Once Freelogic.in is live and offering subscriptions:

  • General Inquiries/Feedback: We aim to respond within 1-2 business days.
  • Technical Support (for subscribed users): Response times will vary based on the subscription tier and issue severity, typically ranging from a few business hours to 1 business day.

Scope of Support (Post-Launch)

Support for the Freelogic.in CRM will generally cover:

  • Assistance with account setup and configuration.
  • Guidance on using the features of the CRM.
  • Troubleshooting and bug fixes related to the Freelogic.in platform.
  • Clarifications on billing and subscription management.

Support will not typically cover:

  • Custom software development or modifications beyond the standard Freelogic.in features.
  • General freelance business advice (though we aim to provide helpful resources!).
  • Issues with third-party services you might use alongside Freelogic.in (e.g., your email provider, accounting software, unless it's a direct integration we support).
  • Direct support for your clients or projects managed within the CRM; Freelogic.in is a tool for you to manage them.

Feedback & Feature Requests

We highly value your feedback and ideas for improving Freelogic.in! Please feel free to share your suggestions via the contact methods above. While we can't implement every request, user input is crucial for the evolution of the platform.

Documentation (Future)

Upon launch, we plan to provide comprehensive documentation, tutorials, and FAQs to help you get the most out of Freelogic.in.
